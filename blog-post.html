Freshman Year at Northwestern

By Sebastian Perez-DelgadoAug 27, 2019

I knew very little about this school when applying to colleges and was on the verge of not even going to this school. I am glad I ended up going.

Some Context

I knew I wanted to go to college to study computer science I just didn't know what school to apply to. Sure I had a dream school, Stanford regect over here, but I knew that I had to keep my options open and apply to schools where I have a shot. 
Here I am sitting down using google when I found Northwestern. It's a good engineering school, in a location I'm familiar with, and has a slightly higher acceptance rate. Okay cool, This can be my back up if I don't get in Standford or UChicago.
